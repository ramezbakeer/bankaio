Frequently Ask Questions

Find answers to common questions about our banking app. If you need further assistance, our support team is here to help!

How do I access the banking app?

To access the banking app, you must first be a client of the bank. If youâ€™re already a client, you can log in using your registered email and password.

What if I am not a bank client?

If you are not a client yet, please visit your nearest bank branch to open an account. Once you have completed the registration process, you will receive login credentials to access the app.

How can I transfer money between accounts?

To transfer money, log in to your account, navigate to the "Transfer" section, and select the accounts you wish to transfer funds between. Enter the amount and any notes, then confirm the transaction.

How can I check my transaction history?

You can check your transaction history by logging into your account and going to the "Transaction History" section. Here, you can view, filter, and download your transactions for a specific period.

How do I pay my bills using the app?

To pay your bills, log in to your account and navigate to the "Bills" section. Select the biller you want to pay, enter the amount, and confirm the payment. You can also set up recurring payments for regular bills.

How do I update my personal information?

To update your personal information, log in to your account and go to the "Profile" or "Settings" section. You can edit your details such as your phone number, email address, and other personal information.

What happens if I exceed my account balance?

If you attempt to make a transaction that exceeds your available balance, the transaction will typically be declined. Ensure you have sufficient funds before making any transactions.

What security measures do you have in place?

We implement multiple layers of security, including encryption, two-factor authentication, and real-time fraud detection. Your data is stored securely, and we continuously monitor for suspicious activity.

How do I close my account?

To close your account, please contact customer support through our email. They will guide you through the process and inform you of any necessary steps and potential fees.

Is there a mobile app available?

Currently, we offer a web application. However, we are working on a mobile app for easier access. Stay tuned for updates!
